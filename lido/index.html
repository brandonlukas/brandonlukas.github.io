01 — Timed

The program plays itself.

Write the workout once. Lido runs it with voice cues — what's next, halfway, five seconds left — so your phone stays in your pocket and your hands stay on the floor.

02 — Sets

Rest, counted for you.

For gym workouts you run yourself: tap when a set ends and Lido times the rest, chiming when it's time to lift again. Your sets, reps, and rest are remembered between visits.

03 — The pool

Every finish floats a toy in.

The month keeps its own record: finish a workout and its toy drifts into the pool. A heavy month earns a grander pool — two ladders, a diving board, the works.

Nothing counts streaks.

And nothing is ever red because you rested. Lido keeps a record, not a score — a missed day is simply water.

Share a workout

Send a program to a friend as a link. It opens in their Lido, ready to add.

Private by construction

Everything lives on your phone — no account, no ads, no analytics. Shared workouts travel inside the link itself and never touch a server.
